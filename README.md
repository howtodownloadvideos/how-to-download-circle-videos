# How to Download Circle Videos for Free Chrome Browser Extension Method


Circle Downloader is a browser extension for saving authorized videos from Circle community, course, and academy pages.

This step-by-step guide follows the Chrome extension method from the video.

> Only download Circle videos you own, have permission to save, or are legally allowed to access offline.

## What Is Circle Downloader?

Circle Downloader detects supported video resources on Circle pages and lets you save them from the extension panel or from a convenient video download button.

Verified repository:

```text
https://github.com/serpapps/circle-downloader
```

## Before You Start

You will need:

- Google Chrome.
- Access to a Circle page with a video you are authorized to save.
- The release asset `circle-downloader-chrome.zip`.
- An email address for one-time verification.

## Step 1: Find the Repository

Search Google for:

```text
serpapps circle downloader
```

Open the GitHub repository for `serpapps/circle-downloader`.

## Step 2: Download the Chrome ZIP

Open **Releases** and choose the latest release.

For Chrome, download:

```text
circle-downloader-chrome.zip
```

Other verified browser ZIPs include `circle-downloader-edge.zip` and `circle-downloader-brave.zip`.

## Step 3: Extract the ZIP

Extract `circle-downloader-chrome.zip` and keep the extracted folder ready.

## Step 4: Load It in Chrome

Open:

```text
chrome://extensions
```

Enable **Developer mode**, click **Load unpacked**, and select the extracted extension folder.

## Step 5: Pin the Extension

Use the Chrome puzzle-piece menu to pin Circle Downloader to the toolbar.

## Step 6: Verify Your Email

Open the extension, enter your email, request the code, and paste the code into the extension to verify.

## Step 7: Open a Circle Video Page

Navigate to the Circle page, lesson, or community post that contains the video.

Click play if needed. In the walkthrough, playing the video helps make it visible to the extension.

## Step 8: Select the Video Resource

Open Circle Downloader from the toolbar. It may show multiple resources, including text, images, and videos.

Choose the video resource you want to save. The walkthrough example shows a video quality such as 1920 x 1080.

## Step 9: Click Download

Click the download button for the video. Chrome may ask where you want to save the file.

The extension shows progress while the video downloads.

## Troubleshooting Tips

- Click play before opening the extension if the video does not appear.
- Make sure you select the video resource, not images or text.
- Check that you installed from `circle-downloader-chrome.zip`.
- Keep the page open until the progress indicator finishes.

## FAQ

### Can I use the video button instead of the extension panel?

Yes. The walkthrough mentions a convenient download button at the top-right of videos.

### Why does the extension show more than one resource?

Circle pages can contain several resource types. Select the video entry you want.

### Where does the file download?

It saves to your browser's configured download location.

## Final Thoughts

Install `circle-downloader-chrome.zip`, verify the extension, open the Circle video page, play the video if needed, select the detected video resource, and click download.
